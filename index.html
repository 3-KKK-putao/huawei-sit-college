学院简介

中文简介

华为－沈工现代产业学院是沈阳工学院与华为共建的ICT人才培养全球样板点，于2024年建成，总面积超5000平方米。学院设有26间专业实验室，涵盖鸿蒙开发、昇腾人工智能、鲲鹏云计算、5G通信等前沿领域，并配套VUE考试中心；配备华为昇腾GPU服务器、核心交换机、鸿蒙智能实践台、大模型实践箱等数十种先进设备，构建了教学、科研、实践一体化的创新平台。

同时建有2间星辰联合创新实验室，具备全栈能力、边学边练、六位一体等特色，融合多重仿真、真实案例与自动测评，依托智能物联边缘计算平台支持场景叠加与以赛促学。

学院依托华为自主技术体系，深化产教融合，通过政校企协同提升社会服务能力，面向在校生及社会人员开设华为认证培训、场景化项目实践及数字化领导力研修班，培养ICT复合型与数字化管理人才。

English Introduction

Huawei-SIT Modern Industrial College

Huawei-SIT Modern Industrial College is a global demonstration site for ICT talent cultivation, jointly established by Shenyang Institute of Technology and Huawei. Completed in 2024, it covers an area of over 5,000 square meters. The college features 26 specialized laboratories in cutting-edge fields such as HarmonyOS development, Ascend AI, Kunpeng cloud computing, and 5G communication, along with a VUE exam center. It is also equipped with a wide range of advanced facilities, including Huawei Ascend GPU servers, core switches, HarmonyOS smart experiment platforms, and large-model practice kits, creating an integrated innovation platform for teaching, research, and practice.

The college also houses two Xingchen Joint Innovation Labs, which feature full-stack capabilities, hands-on learning, and an integrated six-in-one system. These labs combine multi-simulation, real-world cases, and automated assessment, powered by a smart IoT edge computing platform for scenario-based learning and competition-driven training.

Based on Huawei's proprietary technology system, the college promotes deeper industry-education integration and enhances its social service capacity through government-enterprise-university collaboration. It offers Huawei certification training, scenario-based project practice, and digital leadership workshops for both current students and professionals to cultivate ICT professionals with interdisciplinary skills and digital management talents.

国家级大创项目

"一院一卡"：沈工产业学院双语文化名片设计与数字传播

本数字名片是沈阳工学院大学生创新创业训练计划项目《"一院一卡"：沈工产业学院双语文化名片设计与数字传播》的成果之一。项目为全校28个产业学院设计制作中英双语文化名片，探索"AI辅助+人工审校"的双语内容生产模式，服务产教融合成果的数字化传播。

This digital brochure is an outcome of the College Student Innovation and Entrepreneurship Training Program project titled "One College, One Card": Bilingual Cultural Identity Design and Digital Communication for SIT's Industrial Colleges. The project designs bilingual cultural cards for 28 industrial colleges at SIT, exploring an "AI-assisted + human-edited" model for bilingual content production to support the digital dissemination of industry-education integration achievements.
